The Movie Game

The Movie Game was created with React, Redux, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Passport.js, and The Movie Database's third party API. It tests users knowledge of movie titles while reminding them of their favorite game to play on road trips. Users attempt to think of as many movies as they can that start with the provided letter.
